Evenement Haunted By Daylight

Horror lovers rejoice, it’s your time to shine. Halloween season, where it’s not a success unless the fear is flowing through you. Celebrate the spookiest month of the year in Dead by Daylight. We’re bringing plenty of treats – and a few tricks – including adrenaline-pumping gameplay challenges, an exciting Event, a wickedly macabre Tome starring The Ghost Face and Mikaela Reid, and much more.
